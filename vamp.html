Marceline Abadeer (the Vampire Queen) is a major character in the cartoon show, Adventure Time, who was initially seen as an enemy to Finn and Jake, but quickly became one of their closest friends.

Marceline is a millenium old half-demon/half-vampire that's often described as a "wild rocker girl". As an extremely talented musician and songwriter, Marceline's music stands as some of Adventure Time's most popular hits.

Basic Powers

  • Levitation
  • Pyrokinesis
  • Telekinesis
  • Invisibility
  • Necromancy
  • Rapid healing
  • Shape-shifting
  • Eating shades of red

Famous Quote:
